TJ Yuson's personal portfolio is an unexpected, time-sensitive project that was assigned to the Web Development Team during the final week of my internship. Mr. TJ Yuson, our CEO, wanted us to design his own portfolio, which would include all of his accomplishments, brands, and advocacies. This project conflicts with the release timetable of the Yuson Group of Companies, which was still in the works at the time. To manage the increasing workload, I separated the team into two groups, taking into account each member's talents and strengths. Both websites were already available on the internet when I finished my internship.
